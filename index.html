About Me

Hi, this is Shishir!

I am from Patna, India. My long-term goal is to use data science to help the Government of India identify citizens in dire need of help and efficiently deliver essential services.

I am currently pursuing my Master's of Science degree in Data Science (MSDS) at the University of San Francisco, where I am in the top 5% of my class with a GPA of 3.97.

Before joining USF, I worked as a Data Scientist at Loylty Rewardz . Here, I deployed machine learning algorithms to help our banking clients understand the behavior of their card holding customers and implemented strategies to increase customer stickiness.
